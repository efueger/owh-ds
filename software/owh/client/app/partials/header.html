The Office on Women's Health
Quick Health Data Online
The factors contributing to women’s overall health are wide-reaching and often surprising, yet understanding them can be critical. This interactive site makes available state and country wide health data so that researchers, students, policy makers, grant writers and men and women across the country can fully explore the key issues and concerns of women’s health.
 
On This Site You Can:

Access dozens of datasets published by the Federal Government and gain insight into the main topics concerning women’s health today.

Compare health information and statistics across populations, filtering for factors such as sex, age, race, and other socioeconomic variables.

Generate custom reports, graphs and maps to visually represent a wide range of information.
